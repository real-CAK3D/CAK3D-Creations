What CAK3D-Creations is

A small creative engineering shop with a mechanic’s brain and a maker’s bench.

Most of these projects started as “I need this for my own setup.” A device needed a dashboard. A family app needed NFC tags. A trail tool needed offline maps. A business idea needed payments. CAK3D-Creations collects those builds into one workshop identity.

This page is built to grow into a real business front door, but it stays honest about the current stage: these are active personal projects, prototypes, and working systems being shaped into products and services over time.

Why the device work matters

The dashboards are not decoration. They are how the workshop sees the machines: uptime, services, app previews, NFC tools, home lab experiments, and Raspberry Pi workflows.

  • Simple telemetry dashboards for fast device awareness.
  • Specialist agents for device lanes and low-clutter coordination.
  • Phone-first interfaces for inventory, hiking, pet ID, and shop tools.
  • Vercel/Supabase paths when a prototype needs to leave the LAN.

Stripe-ready, not secret-leaky

Payments will be wired behind a backend boundary.

When CAK3D-Creations starts selling builds, consults, downloads, support, or subscriptions, Stripe Checkout should be created through Vercel serverless functions or Stripe Payment Links — never by putting Stripe secret keys in this static frontend.
